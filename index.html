A World Built by the Community

FAQ

Do I need a paid Minecraft account to join? ▼
No! BlockBound SMP is cracked-friendly. You can join with any launcher, including free ones like TLauncher or SKLauncher. Just add the server IP and port and you're in.
Can Bedrock and Java players play together? ▼
Yes! We support cross-play between Java and Bedrock editions. Both use the same IP blockbound.mc-play.org and the same port 20017.
Will the world ever be reset? ▼
World resets only happen between seasons, and we always announce them well in advance on Discord. Season 1 is currently active with no reset planned. Join our Discord to stay updated.
How do I report a player or appeal a ban? ▼
All reports and ban appeals are handled through our Discord server. Do not argue in-game — open a ticket on Discord and staff will review it promptly.
Is PvP allowed? ▼
Yes, PvP is enabled on the server. However, spawn camping, repeatedly targeting the same player, or using PvP to harass others is not allowed and may result in a punishment.
How do I apply for staff? ▼
Staff applications are open on our Discord server. Be an active and positive community member — that's the best way to get noticed!
What Minecraft version should I use? ▼
We recommend using the latest stable version of Minecraft for the best experience. Older versions may work but are not guaranteed to be fully compatible.
